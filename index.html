FAQ

How often is the data updated?
Two cadences. Liveness is re-verified weekly — every Monday we re-check the whole set, refresh each shop's last-seen date, flag anything gone missing two weeks running, and web-verify closures before removing them, with a live weekly change feed of openings and closures. The underlying open datasets refresh on their own cadence, roughly quarterly.
Can I actually use this legally?
Yes. Built from public and open data — we don't scrape Tabelog or copy Google Places. An original, independently-derived work.
What format is the API response?
JSON over REST, plus an MCP server. Schema documentation on signup.
